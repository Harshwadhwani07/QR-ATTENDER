FAQs

Why to use our app?

When students improve their attendance rates, they improve their academic prospects and chances for graduating. Our services helps them to stay on their track.

Can students manipulate the attendance time as well as other data?

No, student cannot manipulate the data. The attendance system takes time set in the attendance device. Instead of device student can submit attendance from web browser. If it happens then there are triggers in our database by which we will know which data is manipulated/deleted.

What about attendance data export system?

We have Attendance data export system which pulls the attendance logs from database and upload into excel sheet .From where you can download it.
